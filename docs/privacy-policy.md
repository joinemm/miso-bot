# Privacy Policy

This Privacy Policy explains how Miso Bot processes personal data when you use the bot or add it to a Discord server.

This policy covers data processed by Miso Bot itself. Discord and third-party services used through Miso Bot process data under their own terms and privacy policies.

## Controller

Miso Bot is operated by Joonas Rautiola.

Contact:

- Email: `mail@joinemm.dev`

If you have questions about this policy or want to exercise your data protection rights, contact the address above and include your Discord user ID if possible.

## Data Miso Bot processes

Depending on which features are used, Miso Bot may process the following categories of personal data:

- Discord identifiers and context, such as user IDs, guild IDs, channel IDs, message links, and timestamps
- Data you submit to use features, such as notification keywords, reminder text, custom command content, profile descriptions, background URLs, timezone, location string, sunsign, and linked Last.fm username
- Feature preferences, such as Last.fm voting settings and whether a compliance notice has been acknowledged
- User-created media references, such as source URLs for GIF creation
- Activity and statistics, such as command usage counters, typing test history, typing race results, notification trigger counts, fishy game progress, and artist crown standings
- Moderation and access-control records, such as guild blacklists, global blacklists, Last.fm ranking blacklists, mute records, VIP status, and premium server management links
- Donation and entitlement records, such as donation platform, external platform username, donation amount, donation tier, active donor status, donation dates, and premium/VIP eligibility
- Social feature data, such as marriage status between two Discord users and the marriage date

Miso Bot does not store normal message content by default. However, message content may be processed transiently in order to provide features such as keyword notifications and typing tests. If a user has subscribed to a notification keyword, part of a matching message may be forwarded to that user in a direct message together with a jump link to the original message.

Please do not submit sensitive personal data through free-text features unless it is necessary and you understand the risks.

## Where the data comes from

Miso Bot receives personal data:

- directly from you when you run commands or configure features
- from Discord as part of normal bot operation
- from server administrators or the bot operator when moderation, premium, donor, or access-control actions are taken
- from third-party services you choose to use with Miso Bot, such as Last.fm or GIPHY

## Why Miso Bot processes personal data

Miso Bot processes personal data in order to:

- provide the commands and features you ask to use
- store your settings and preferences
- maintain reminders, notifications, profiles, minigame progress, rankings, and other persistent features
- enforce moderation, abuse-prevention, anti-cheat, and service integrity controls
- manage premium, VIP, and donor entitlements
- generate public or server-visible feature outputs such as leaderboards, profiles, crowns, marriage status, and similar user-facing results
- troubleshoot issues, maintain the service, and monitor overall feature usage

## Legal bases

Depending on the context, Miso Bot generally relies on the following legal bases under the GDPR:

- performance of a contract or taking steps at your request, where you actively use a feature and the processing is needed to provide it
- legitimate interests, such as keeping the bot secure, preventing abuse, enforcing moderation, maintaining service functionality, and managing donor or premium entitlements
- consent or clearly optional user choice, where you voluntarily provide optional profile, notification, linking, or preference data and can later remove it

## Sharing and recipients

Personal data may be shared with:

- Discord, because Miso Bot operates on the Discord platform
- infrastructure and hosting services used to run the bot and its database
- third-party services that are necessary for features you choose to use, such as Last.fm, GIPHY, Google Translate, WolframAlpha, and similar integrations
- other Discord users where that is part of the feature itself

Examples of user-visible sharing include:

- keyword notifications, where matching message content is sent to the subscribing user
- leaderboards, crowns, and profile-related outputs shown in Discord
- marriage status shown through the related command
- active donor listings, where the bot may publish a donor's Discord username, avatar, and donation amount through public web output

Miso Bot does not sell personal data.

## International transfers

Because Discord and some third-party service providers may process data outside the EEA, personal data may be transferred outside the EEA when you use Miso Bot or connected integrations. Where this happens, the relevant provider is responsible for its own processing under its own privacy documentation.

## Retention

Miso Bot does not use one identical retention period for all categories of data. Instead, data is kept for as long as it is needed for the feature or purpose it was collected for, using the following criteria:

- settings, linked accounts, reminders, notifications, profile customisation, custom commands, and similar feature data are kept until you remove them, request deletion, or they are no longer needed for the feature
- gameplay, statistics, history, and ranking data are kept until you delete them where the feature allows it, request deletion, or the data is no longer needed
- moderation, blacklist, mute, anti-abuse, and access-control records are kept until they are removed by authorised staff or are no longer needed for security or moderation purposes
- donor, premium, VIP, and related administrative records are kept for as long as they are needed to manage entitlements, resolve disputes, maintain internal records, or comply with legal obligations

If you ask for deletion, some data may need to be retained longer where retention is necessary to establish, exercise, or defend legal claims, or to comply with a legal obligation.

## Your rights

Subject to applicable law, you may have the right to:

- access your personal data
- request correction of inaccurate data
- request deletion of your data
- request restriction of processing
- object to processing based on legitimate interests
- receive a copy of data you provided, where the right to data portability applies
- withdraw consent where processing is based on consent
- lodge a complaint with your local supervisory authority

If you are in Finland, you can also complain to the Office of the Data Protection Ombudsman.

## How to request access or deletion

You can contact `mail@joinemm.dev` to request access to or deletion of your data. To help locate your records, include:

- your Discord user ID
- the servers where you used Miso Bot, if relevant
- the feature or data category your request concerns, if known

Miso Bot may need to verify that the request is really from you before acting on it.

## Changes to this policy

This policy may be updated from time to time to reflect changes to Miso Bot, its integrations, or applicable law. The latest version published in the repository or on the project website is the version that applies.
